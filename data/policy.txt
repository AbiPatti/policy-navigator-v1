GLOBAL TECH SOLUTIONS - REMOTE WORK POLICY (2025)

1. PURPOSE
This policy outlines the guidelines for employees working remotely. Our goal is to balance flexibility with productivity.

2. ELIGIBILITY
- Full-time employees are eligible for "Hybrid Remote" status after 90 days of employment.
- "Fully Remote" status requires VP-level approval.
- Interns must work from the HQ at least 4 days a week.

3. WORK HOURS & AVAILABILITY
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST. You must be online on Slack/Teams during these hours.
- Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.
- Unavailability for more than 2 hours during the workday requires manager notification.

4. EQUIPMENT & SECURITY
- The company provides a MacBook Pro and a $500 stipend for home office setup.
- Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal servers.
- Working from public Wi-Fi (cafes, airports) is strictly prohibited unless using the VPN.

5. EXPENSES
- Internet bills are NOT reimbursed.
- Travel to HQ for quarterly "All-Hands" meetings is 100% covered by the company.

6. TERMINATION OF REMOTE STATUS
- The company reserves the right to revoke remote privileges if performance drops below expectations for two consecutive quarters.
